# Web Dashboard

**URL:** [data.autoperp.app](https://data.autoperp.app)
**Status:** Coming Soon

The AutoPerp web dashboard provides a visual interface for monitoring your trading performance, viewing trade history, and tracking your portfolio.

## Features (Coming Soon)

### Equity Curve
Track your account balance over time with an interactive equity curve. See drawdowns, recovery periods, and overall growth at a glance.

### Trade History
Full table of all executed trades with:
- Entry and exit prices
- P&L per trade ($ and %)
- Confidence score at entry
- Close reason (TP1, trailing SL, manual, circuit breaker)
- Duration
- Direction (Long/Short)

### Open Positions
Real-time view of currently open positions:
- Current price vs entry price
- Unrealized P&L
- Stop loss and take profit levels
- Time in trade

### Strategy Status
Live view of the V6 engine:
- Current regime classification per token
- Last scan time
- Pending signals
- Active confidence scores

### Performance Analytics
- Daily, weekly, monthly P&L summaries
- Win rate and average R:R
- Best and worst performing tokens
- Performance by risk profile
- Direction breakdown (Long vs Short)

### Auto-Refresh
Dashboard updates every 15 seconds automatically. No manual refreshing needed.

## Access

The dashboard uses Telegram authentication — log in with the same Telegram account you use with the bot. Your subscription tier determines access level.

## Mobile

The dashboard is fully responsive and works on mobile browsers. No app download required.
