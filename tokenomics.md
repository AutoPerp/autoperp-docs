## Revenue-Backed Buybacks

AutoPerp generates revenue from trading subscription fees. **50% of all revenue** is used to buy back $APR from the open market.

### How It Works

1. AutoPerp collects subscription fees from Starter ($25/mo), Pro ($50/mo), and Lifetime ($500) plans
2. 50% of that revenue is allocated to the buyback pool
3. Buybacks are executed on the open market at regular intervals
4. Bought-back tokens are removed from circulating supply

### Why Buybacks?

- **Direct price support** — Consistent buy pressure from real revenue
- **Reducing supply** — Fewer tokens in circulation over time
- **Real yield** — Not inflationary rewards. Backed by actual protocol income.
- **Transparent** — All buyback transactions are verifiable on-chain on Solana

## What $APR Is NOT

- **Not a governance token** (yet) — Governance features may be added later
- **No VC allocation** — No venture capital unlock schedules
- **No complex vesting** — Straightforward tokenomics
- **Not required to use AutoPerp** — You can subscribe and trade without holding $APR
