## Payment

All payments are handled via crypto:

- **SOL** — Solana native token
- **USDC** — On Solana

To subscribe:
1. Type `/subscribe` in [@autoperpbot](https://t.me/autoperpbot)
2. Choose your plan
3. Send the exact amount to the generated payment address
4. Confirmation is automatic — your subscription activates immediately

## Upgrading

You can upgrade your plan at any time by running `/subscribe` again and selecting a higher tier. The new plan activates immediately.
